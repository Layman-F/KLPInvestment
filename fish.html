Fish



KLP Investment has 3 dams on the farm which are mainly for fisheries. The types of Fish
found in these dams include Chiloglanis emarginatus,African butter catfish,Silver Labeo,
Spotted killifish,Astatotilapia calliptera,Leaden labeo just but a few to mention. The fish in
these ponds are ordered from the great Zambezi lake as small fries and are supplied leaves
of river tamarind and sweet potato leaves. Chicken droppings too are tied in a sack and placed on the ponds as
promote the growth of algae which fish feed on.
