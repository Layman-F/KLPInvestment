Chicken





Chickens are the main part of the Kensington Livestock Palace project.
The project was initially for rearing chickens(broilers) for market
distribution but was later expanded to include some of the common livestock in Zimbabwe.


Currently there are plenty of roadrunners which are being reared for the sake of their strong adaptation to
the harsh weather characterised of storms and strong winds which usually takes place around this time of the year.
There are also layers which have been included in the farm to increase productivity as eggs are some of the
agricultural products with a very high market
