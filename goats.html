Goats





KLP Investment has some healthy looking goats. The goats are bred mainly for meat and
milk purposes.


Two indegenous goat types that exist in the country also happen to be the two indegenous types found
in KLP Investment.

the Mashona

The Mashona goat wish short horizontal ears,strong horns, short hair and have small legs and frames. The adult
goat weighs 30kg liveweight and is about 60cm tall at the withers.


the Matebele

The Matebele has long ears, some pendulous with turned up tips, and many with an intermediate type of ear,
varied hairiness and a wide range in coat colour. Adult liveweight for males is 50 to 55kg and females 39kg.
